# Week 01

## Guidance notes

Use this file to document your engagement with *Experiment 1: Data Drawings* for the Making Journal assignment.

The Making Journal is a record of your experiments during the first phase of the course. It is a space to document and reflect on your experiences working with different tools and techniques for designing with data.

Each experiment corresponds to a comprehensive entry in your journal. You will document your engagement with in-class and independent study workshop tasks, alongside written reflections and responses to specific design projects and readings. 

To capture the full scope of your practice, each entry must include a mix of visual and textual evidence, such as sketches, screenshots, GIFs, diagrams, process notes, instructions and reflections.

Use headings and appropriate Markdown formatting to structure your entry.

Make sure to include **ALL** components of the experiment, that is, both in-class and independent study tasks.

These guidance notes should not appear in your final journal entry.
